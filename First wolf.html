The First Wolf

The Start of the Evolutionairy Adventure

March 29, 2016 Victor Szwarcberg

The Evolution of the wolf is clouded of mystery that still biologists don't understand. Still many believe that they came from a primitive carnivores known as miacids. These pre-canines ranged from gopher-sized to dog-sized animals. They seemed to appear in the Lower Teriary about fifty two million years ago. They had evolved from Creaceous (dinosaurs).
